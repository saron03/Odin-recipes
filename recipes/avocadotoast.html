Avocado Toast Recipe

Description

Avocado toast is a simple and healthy breakfast or snack option. With creamy mashed avocado spread over crispy toast, it's both delicious and nutritious. Enjoy it plain or customize with your favorite toppings for a quick, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice(optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread to your desired level of crispiness.
  2. While the bread is toasting, mash the avocado in a bowl.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread.
  4. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a splash of lemon juice if desired.
  5. Serve immediately.
